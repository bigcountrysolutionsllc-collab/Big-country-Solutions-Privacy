1. Introduction

Big Country Solution LLC ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. By using our Services, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Notice.

2. Definitions

"Personal Information" means any information that identifies or could reasonably identify a specific individual, including but not limited to name, email address, phone number, mailing address, driver's license number, payment information, and any other data you voluntarily provide.

"Sensitive Information" means driver's license numbers, payment card information, government-issued identification, financial data, background check results, and similar data requiring heightened protection.

3. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of Personal Information: Registration Data (name, email, phone number, mailing address), Identification (driver's license number and state of issuance), Payment Information (credit card, debit card, or bank account details processed securely and never stored in full), Address Information (residential and business addresses), Background Check Data, Communications (messages, support tickets, and inquiries), Event Registration data, and User-Generated Content (posts, comments, reviews).

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your Personal Information solely for: providing and improving our Services, processing transactions and payments, verifying your identity and conducting legally required background checks, responding to inquiries and providing customer support, sending transactional communications, complying with valid court orders and lawful subpoenas, and preventing fraud. We do NOT use your information for marketing or advertising without your explicit written consent.

5. Data Retention and Storage

All Personal Information is retained and stored exclusively within our secure, encrypted systems. Information is kept only as long as necessary or as required by law. Once your account is closed or a transaction is complete, we securely delete or anonymize your data within ninety (90) days, except where legally required to retain records.

6. Strict Non-Sharing Policy

Your Personal Information will NOT be shared with third parties under any circumstances, except as explicitly outlined below.

6.1 Legal Compliance: We will disclose your information only in response to a valid court order, judicial subpoena, or warrant. We will NOT voluntarily disclose information to law enforcement without valid legal process.

6.2 Background Checks: We share only the minimum necessary information with our third-party background check provider, bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

6.3 Payment Processing: Payment information is processed through secure, PCI-DSS compliant processors. We do not store full payment card details.

6.4 No Other Sharing: We will never sell, rent, license, or trade your Personal Information to any third party.

7. Data Security

Your information is protected through industry-standard encryption, secure servers, and access controls. Sensitive Information is encrypted both in transit and at rest using AES-256 encryption or equivalent. Only authorized employees with a legitimate business need have access to your data.
